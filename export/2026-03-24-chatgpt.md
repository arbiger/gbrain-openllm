# 關於ChatGPT   你應該這樣用

> **TLDR:** ChatGPT使用技巧：不要直接問問題，而是讓AI反過來問你問題。先用語音輸入詳細說明你的問題，然後對AI說「現在問我一個問題，等我回答後再問下一個」。這樣AI就變成一個教練，幫助你思考而不是直接幫你想答案。

---

My favorite use of an AI assistant
that very few use is don't ask it questions
Ask it to ask you questions instead
Explain whatever your problem is
By the way, do this in voice-to-text
Because when you use voice-to-text
You will find yourself being much more explicit
than giving the AI much more context on the problem
That you might have in front of you
And then finish your explanation with now ask me
One question at a time
Waiting for my answer in between
And now you basically created
Some kind of executive coach type conversation
Where the AI is going to start asking you questions
And help you think
Rather than do the thinking for
